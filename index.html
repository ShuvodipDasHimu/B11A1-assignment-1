Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Bangladesh has emerging startups, digital hubs, and local innovations gaining international recognition.

Sustainability

Renewable energy projects and eco-friendly practices are gradually shaping Bangladesh’s sustainable development.

Infrastructure

Modern bridges, highways, and metro rail projects are transforming connectivity in Bangladesh.

Empowerment

Education programs and microfinance initiatives are uplifting underprivileged communities across Bangladesh.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

The rapid adoption of digital tools is reshaping industries and improving services in Bangladesh.

Green Energy

Bangladesh is advancing renewable energy efforts, focusing on sustainable solutions to meet growing power demands.

Modern Infrastructure

Ongoing development of transport networks and urban facilities is enhancing mobility and boosting economic activities.

"To build Bangladesh 2.0, we must embrace innovation and inclusivity, ensuring that every citizen, regardless of their background, has the opportunity to contribute meaningfully to our collective future and prosperity. By harnessing the power of technology and social entrepreneurship, we can create a sustainable economy that uplifts the marginalized and empowers the youth. It is essential that we foster a culture of collaboration, where ideas flow freely and where every individual feels valued and heard in the journey toward a more equitable society."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
